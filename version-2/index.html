Hi! I’m Ksenia, and this is my project about mindfulness which I created with my friends in 2017. It was a mailing list that teaches how to live a calm and happy life.

It’s like an advent calendar only instead of sweets you have a short easy task for each day of the month, such as meditating for 5 minutes, do not use your phone for one day, being grateful, etc. Most of these tasks can be turned into a habit.

I used Mailchimp, but unfortunately, they suspended all Russian accounts. So, the mailing list isn’t working now. But the letters still are available and I listed them here with auto-translation.
